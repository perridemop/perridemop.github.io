My Projects

Sam Noble 3D Scanning Iniative

The Sam Noble Museum of Natural History presented to our team their challenge to deploy 3D scans throughout their museum. Through these scans, they hope to increase patronage visitation and enhance a patron's experience with artifacts in an exhibit. During the course of our project, we researched different 3D scanning methods before proposing photogrammetry to Sam Noble. Once our client accepted our proposal, hardware and software were purchased to complete a scan. We were able to provide a proof of concept to Sam Noble by creating 3 completed scans of artifacts of varying sizes. We also created user guides for them to create new scans after our project to eventually reach their final goal of deploying the scans throughout the museum.

Database Creation

The purpose of this project was for our team to create a functioning database on Microsoft SQL Server. We began by creating an ERD (Entity Relationship Diagram) normalizing all of the relations, and created a data dictionary before creating the database on Microsoft SQL Server. The database allowed our "client" to store information about their skis and edit where necessary. The "client" requested specific reports to be queried from the data and we were able to produce those reports.
